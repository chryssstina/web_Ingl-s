    About the Team

    We are students in the 2nd semester (2023.2) of the Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at IFCE Campus Iguatu - Cajazeiras Unit. The team is made up of Maria Chrystina, Francisco Vinicius, José Carlos and Maria Eduarda, where together we made this site as a way of relating the Instrumental English subject, taught by Prof. Vilmar, and presenting great names who were and still are part of computing. In this way, we aim to blend these two worlds and share knowledge with more people who are interested in learning more about both.

Great names in computing

Internet, video games, social media, laptops and smartphones - our daily lives have changed dramatically with the advent and development of technology-based products. But we wouldn't have these things without the people who wrote the code, built the machines and guided the evolution of the computer technologies we so easily take for granted today.

But who exactly are these people? The history of computing is full of men and women from different fields who have contributed to building the information age as we know it. Here are some of the names that made history!
