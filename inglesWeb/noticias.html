    About the Team

    We are students in the 2nd semester (2023.2) of the Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at IFCE Campus Iguatu - Cajazeiras Unit. The team is made up of Maria Chrystina, Francisco Vinicius, José Carlos and Maria Eduarda, where together we made this site as a way of relating the Instrumental English subject, taught by Prof. Vilmar, and presenting great names who were and still are part of computing. In this way, we aim to blend these two worlds and share knowledge with more people who are interested in learning more about both.
